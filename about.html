About Us

Holy Family NP Clinic. – Revolutionizing Virtual Healthcare

Holy Family NP Clinic. is dedicated to transforming healthcare through innovative telehealth solutions. Our mission is to bridge the gap in healthcare accessibility, offering high-quality virtual care to individuals across Ontario. By integrating advanced medical technologies and patient-centered approaches, we strive to enhance wellness and improve health outcomes.

Meet Lifin Parakkal Thomas – The Vision Behind Holy Family NP Clinic.

Mr. Lifin Parakkal Thomas is a Canadian and American Board-Certified Nurse Practitioner with a Master’s Degree in Nursing from D’Youville University in Buffalo. Based in Ontario, he has extensive experience in both acute and primary care. He currently serves in the Internal Medicine department at Niagara Health, managing complex medical cases such as congestive heart failure (CHF), chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), chronic kidney disease (CKD), diabetes, infectious diseases, and other multifaceted conditions.

As the Founder and CEO of Holy Family Nurse Practitioner Clinic, Lifin is dedicated to revolutionizing virtual healthcare and alleviating the strain on Ontario’s healthcare system. His expertise spans family practice, chronic disease management, telehealth, and healthcare innovation. He has cared for patients of all ages, emphasizing preventive care and long-term wellness strategies. His work in telehealth has been instrumental in bridging healthcare gaps, providing virtual medical care to individuals with limited access to in-person services across Ontario.

Lifin is at the forefront of AI-integrated healthcare, leveraging cutting-edge technology to enhance patient care, optimize clinical workflows, and improve healthcare accessibility. Passionate about both acute and primary care, he remains committed to lifelong learning, evidence-based practices, and the advancement of digital health solutions. Beyond clinical practice, he actively engages in healthcare innovation, medical research, and initiatives that expand access to quality care. With a steadfast dedication to professional excellence, he continues to make a meaningful impact in the healthcare community.

Virtual Care Clinic Disclaimer

The services provided by Holy Family Nurse Practitioner Clinic through virtual care are for general medical consultations, follow-ups, and non-emergency healthcare needs. By using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

Not for Emergencies

Virtual consultations are not a substitute for emergency care. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or visit the nearest emergency room.

Technology Limitations

While we strive for high-quality virtual care, technical issues such as poor internet connections may impact service quality.

Accuracy of Information

The effectiveness of virtual consultations depends on accurate and complete information from the patient. Misrepresentation may impact care quality.

Privacy & Security

We comply with strict privacy policies, but virtual communication carries inherent risks, such as unauthorized access or data breaches.

Medical Advice & Prescriptions

Any diagnosis, treatment plan, or prescription provided is based on available information. In-person follow-ups or lab tests may be required.

Geographic Restrictions

Virtual care services are subject to state/province laws. Patients must confirm eligibility based on their location.

By proceeding with a virtual consultation, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted this disclaimer. For any concerns, please contact our clinic before your appointment.
